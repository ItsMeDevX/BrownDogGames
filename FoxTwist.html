Fox Twist

A magical side-scrolling platform adventure.

Overview

Fox Twist is a side-scrolling platform game that follows the adventures of a young fox on a mission to defend the world. An evil fox witch has stolen the staff from the old fox wizard, unleashing chaos across the land. Dash through vibrant levels, dodge traps, defeat enemies, and uncover secrets as you restore the staff and stop the witch’s magic.
